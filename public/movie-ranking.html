The Best Movies According to Adarsh

My top 3 movies of all time

Doctor Strange

A Marvel Movie starring the MCU's famous Sorcerer Supreme.

Inside Out

A Pixar Movie showing the different emotions in a 10 year old girl Riley as lovable characters as she goes through a tough new phase in her life.

Phineas and Ferb through the Second Dimension

Starring everyone's favorite cartoon characters Phineas and Ferb, this movie shows their journey to the 2nd dimension, where they meet their alternate selves. Join them as they try to escape while also fixing the problems of the 2nd dimension :).
